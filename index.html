Blue Monkey Tea




About Us

Opened in October 2002, Blue Monkey Tea is a specialty tea store located in Squirrel Hill neighborhood of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Please note that we have closed our Carnegie location. Our tea shop features over 400 varieties of teas, a huge assortment of teapots and tea gadgets, and international sweets. In addition, we also offer tea classes and tea tastings every month.
